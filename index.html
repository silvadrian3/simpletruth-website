MINDFUL LIVING

Simple Truth is an advertising production outfit which aims to strengthen the collaboration of film and TV production processes based on integrity, transparency and responsible creativity.  

Simple Truth is a group of seasoned and dynamic professionals with diverse skills and experiences ranging from production, directing, cinematography and post-production. Forming a team of story-tellers, we are bound by our brand conviction which is committed to provide the most organized, cost efficient and creative production services for TV commercials, AVPs, documentaries, webisodes, short and full-length materials through our solid production management support system. 

A major part of our vision is our heart for advocacy and corporate social responsibility because we believe in doing good and we celebrate by giving back what we earned as blessings through the years.

OUR COMMITMENT

SimpleTruth exists to inspire mindful living through the content we make.
